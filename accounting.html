ACCOUNTING
Fundamentals of Accounting
Teacher: Ma'am Lai
Class Times: Friday: 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Course: Fundamentals of Accounting
Fundamentals of Accounting is designed to provide an introduction to the basic principles and concepts of accounting, with an emphasis on understanding financial statements, recording transactions, and the role of accounting in business decision-making. Students will learn the fundamental skills needed to analyze and interpret financial data.
