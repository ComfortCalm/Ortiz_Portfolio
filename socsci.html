Course: Social Sciences
Social Sciences is a broad and interdisciplinary field that encompasses the study of human society and the various aspects of human behavior and interaction. Courses in social sciences typically cover a wide range of topics related to society, culture, politics, economics, and more. The specific content of a social sciences course can vary widely depending on the institution and the specific focus of the course.
